Top three activities to do at Mugla

1. Visit the ancient city of Kaunos

Explore the ancient city of Kaunos, which is located in the Dalyan region of Mugla. The city is known for its rock-cut tombs and the beautiful Dalyan River.

2. Swim in Oludeniz

Imagine yourself paragliding over the breathtaking Blue Lagoon in Oludeniz, Fethiye, with crystal-clear waters and soft sand beaches beckoning you below.

3. Wander around in Saklikent

Escape the summer heat and trek through Turkey's refreshing Saklikent Gorge, a hidden paradise carved by icy waters.
